## What you’ll see

- **Positions Value** – current value of open shares
- **Profit/Loss** – realized + unrealized PnL
- **Volume Traded** & **Markets Traded** – lifetime activity
- **Tabs**: *Positions* and *Activity* (latest buys/sells with amounts and timestamps)

## Claims

When a market resolves in your favor, a **Claim** button appears.

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Open Portfolio

    Head to <strong>My Account → Portfolio</strong> to see your open
    positions and activity feed.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Locate resolved markets

    Markets ready to settle display a <strong>Claim</strong> button next to the
    position.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Confirm the claim

    Click <strong>Claim</strong> to credit your balance with the payout. Funds
    arrive as soon as the transaction finalizes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info">
Payouts credit instantly after confirmation. Network fees may apply.
</Callout>
