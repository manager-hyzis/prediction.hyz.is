## Tabs

- **Orders** – open, partially filled, filled, canceled
- **Trades** – executed fills (price, size, side)
- **Deposits** / **Withdrawals** – transfers in and out

## Export CSV

Download your account history for records or taxes:

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Choose a tab or date range

    Filter by tab (Orders, Trades, Deposits, Withdrawals) and, if needed,
    adjust the date range to limit the export.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Click Export CSV

    {process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} generates a CSV containing the rows you
    currently see in the table.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    ### Save securely

    Download and store the file safely for bookkeeping, taxes, or compliance
    reviews.
  </Step>
</Steps>
