## About USDC and Polygon

{process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} uses <a href="https://circle.com/en/usdc" target="_blank">USDC (USD Coin)</a>, a federally regulated "stable coin" backed by the US dollar.

{process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} utilizes USDC on the Polygon network for transactions. By using USDC on Polygon, {process.env.NEXT_PUBLIC_SITE_NAME} ensures fast and reliable transactions, enhancing the overall user experience.

### How to purchase and deposit USDC

USDC is available on most major exchanges, including Coinbase.

If your exchange supports sending or withdrawing to Polygon, we recommend this option for faster and fee-free transactions. Alternatively, you can deposit USDC via the Ethereum network.

### Deposit with Visa or Mastercard

<a href="https://meld.io" target="_blank">Meld.io</a> enables you to buy USDC (on Polygon) using your Visa, Mastercard, and select bank cards. Please be aware that payment options and transaction limits may vary depending on your region.
